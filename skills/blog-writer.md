# Blog Writer Skill

## Brand Voice
- **Tone**: Professional, technically precise, quality-focused
- **Language**: Portuguese (pt-BR) unless specified
- **Audience**: Decision makers in medical device manufacturing

## Article Structure

### 1. Title
- Include primary keyword
- Max 60 characters
- Format: "Como [Verbo] [Benefício] em [Contexto]"

### 2. Introduction (150 words)
- Hook with pain point or statistic
- Promise value
- Include keyword naturally

### 3. Body (800-1200 words)
- 3-5 H2 sections
- Include relevant certifications (ISO 13485, ANVISA, FDA)
- Technical details with accessible explanations
- Internal links to related content

### 4. CTA Section
- Lead magnet offer OR
- Contact/demo request

## SEO Checklist
- [ ] Primary keyword in title, H1, first paragraph
- [ ] Secondary keywords in H2s
- [ ] Meta description (150-160 chars)
- [ ] Alt text for images
- [ ] Internal links (2-3 minimum)

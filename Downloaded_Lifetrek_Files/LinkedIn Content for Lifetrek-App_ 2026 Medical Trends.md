## Content Strategy Notes

### Posting Frequency
- Aim for 2-3 posts per week to maintain visibility without overwhelming your audience
- Mix trend-focused content with product highlights and customer success stories

### Engagement Tips
1. **Ask questions** in your posts to encourage comments
2. **Tag relevant companies** (partners, industry leaders) when appropriate
3. **Use visuals**: Pair these posts with high-quality images of your CNC machines, cleanroom facilities, or finished products
4. **Respond promptly** to all comments to build relationships and increase post visibility
5. **Share industry news** and add your expert perspective to position Lifetrek as a thought leader

### Content Pillars
Based on the research, Lifetrek should focus on these core themes:
- **Precision & Quality**: Emphasize micron-level accuracy, ISO certification, advanced metrology
- **Innovation Enablement**: Position as a partner that helps clients bring cutting-edge designs to life
- **Supply Chain Resilience**: Highlight strategic location, reliability, and responsiveness
- **Technical Expertise**: Showcase deep knowledge of medical-grade materials and complex geometries
- **Future-Ready**: Demonstrate awareness of industry trends and investment in advanced capabilities

### Measurement & Optimization
- Track engagement metrics (likes, comments, shares) for each post
- Identify which themes resonate most with your audience
- A/B test different hooks and calls-to-action
- Use LinkedIn Analytics to understand your audience demographics and adjust content accordingly

# Agentic Content Plan: Instagram Fixed Posts

**Role**: Strategist Agent
**Context**: Lifetrek Medical (Precision CMO, ISO 13485, Brazilian/Global)
**Source Material**: `LIFETREK_RAG_KNOWLEDGE.md` & `generate-linkedin-carousel/index.ts` logic.

## Strategy Overview
**Target Audience**: Medical Device OEMs (Engineering Managers, Procurement).
**Tone**: Professional, authoritative, technically precise, quality-focused.
**Goal**: Establish instant credibility for new visitors.

## Post 1: Identity (Who We Are)
- **Concept**: The "Partner" Angle. Not just a factory, but an extension of the client's team.
- **Key RAG Entities**: "30+ Years Experience", "ISO 13485", "ANVISA".
- **Visual Strategy**: High-end facility exterior, but with a human element (e.g., engineer walking in) to show scale and liveliness.
- **Hook**: "Precision at Scale."

## Post 2: Capabilities (What We Do)
- **Concept**: "End-to-End Control". From Swiss turning to sterile packaging.
- **Key RAG Entities**: "Citizen Cincom L20/L32", "5-Axis", "ISO 7 Cleanroom", "CMM Inspection".
- **Visual Strategy**: Split composition (or carousel slide style). 
    - Top: Macro of Swiss turning (Process).
    - Bottom: Cleanroom packaging (Finish).
- **Hook**: "From Print to Sterile Pack."

## Post 3: Trust (Why Us)
- **Concept**: "Risk Mitigation". The biggest fear of OEMs is recall/failure.
- **Key RAG Entities**: "100% CMM", "Full Traceability", "Material Certificates".
- **Visual Strategy**: A "White Glove" quality check moment. Close up of a CMM probe touching a titanium implant, or a gloved hand holding a finished spinal screw against a technical drawing.
- **Hook**: "Quality is not a department. It's our product."

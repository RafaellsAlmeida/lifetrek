# Social Media Guidelines: LinkedIn & Instagram

**Lifetrek Medical | Technical Content Visual System**

These guidelines define the approved visual frameworks for all social media content. Treat the four templates below as the core visual families. The full approved library is broader and includes multiple variants already present in `GoodPostExemples/`. Every post must anchor to one of these four families or to a documented variant that clearly belongs to one of them.

---

## Universal Visual Rules

- **Brand Consistency**: Always use Corporate Blue (`#004F8F`) as the dominant color.
- **Logo Positioning**: The Lifetrek Medical logo @logo.png (white boxed version) must appear in the **top-right** corner of every slide (except Template D).
- **Background Integrity**: **ALWAYS use real Lifetrek photos** of the facility, machinery, or products. Avoid pure AI-generated backgrounds for final outputs.
- **Background AI-Enhancements**: **If neeeded** use AI to enhance the image (e.g., remove people, add blur, etc.)
- **Typography**: Use **Inter** font family. Text should be white for maximum contrast against dark overlays.
- **Accents**:
  - Innovation Green (`#1A7A3E`) for labels and accents.
  - Energy Orange (`#F07818`) for CTAs.
- **CTA Rule**: **CTAs are prohibited** on all posts except for those specifically announcing **lead magnets** (e.g., whitepapers, webinars, tools). Standard awareness or educational posts should end with a thought-provoking conclusion or "diamond sparkle" instead of a CTA.
- **Typography Variation**: Use a mix of **Bold** and **Regular** (unbold) weights within headlines and body text to provide hierarchy. Emphasize **strategic keywords** with bolding to guide the reader's eye.

## Selection Rule

1. Select the **base family** (A/B/C/D).
2. Select the **specific approved variant** from `GoodPostExemples/` that best matches the topic.
3. Reuse composition logic from that reference.
4. Do not invent a new family or unrelated visual language.

## Template A: "Glassmorphism Card"

*Default for body slides, insights, and CTAs.*

- **Background**: Real photo with a dark blue overlay (`rgba(0,30,70,0.65)`).
- **Card**: Glassmorphism effect, left-aligned, ~65% width. `rgba(8,18,35,0.80)` + backdrop blur.
- **Label**: Small ALL CAPS text above headline in Innovation Green (`#1A7A3E`) (e.g., "INSIGHT", "PASSO A PASSO").
- **Headline**: Inter Bold (~42–48px), white.
- **Body**: Inter Regular (~18–22px), white, max 4 lines.

---

## Template B: "Full-Bleed Dark Text"

*Used for hooks, cover slides, and strong statements.*

- **Background**: Full-bleed real photo with a dark tinted overlay (blue-to-green gradient).
- **Logo**: Top-right with a thin horizontal white rule line below it.
- **Headline**: Extra large, left-aligned. Use a mix of **Bold** and **Regular** weights (e.g., "**TCO 2026**: O novo padrão de produção"). No card background.
- **Footer**:
  - Thin accent line (Green or Orange).
  - Slide counter bottom-left (e.g., "1 de 7").
  - Diamond sparkle `◆` bottom-right.

---

## Template C: "Split Comparison"

*Used for educational content, X vs Y, and before/after.*

- **Layout**: Vertical 50/50 split.
- **Visuals**: Each half features a different color tint plus a full-bleed photo.
- **Typography**: Very large bold labels at the top of each half. No text cards.

---

## Template D: "Pure Photo / Equipment Showcase"

*Used for high-impact machinery and facility highlights.*

- **Visual**: High-quality real photo without text cards or overlays.
- **Subject**: Showcase the equipment (e.g., Zeiss CMM) or facility (Clean Room).
- **Branding**: Brand association is achieved through the quality of the imagery and context. No logo required if the context is clearly branded.
- **Careful**: Use our equipment image as reference images in the AI-generation. Select the equipment that most closely align with the post.

## Working Rule For Agents

- Do not reduce the system to "only four rigid layouts."
- In briefs and prompts, identify both:
  - the base family (`A`, `B`, `C`, or `D`)
  - the closest approved variant in `GoodPostExemples/`
- If a reference appears hybrid, map it to the nearest family and explain that choice in the brief.
